About this home

This beautiful end-of-row townhouse on a desirable corner lot offers 4 bedrooms, 2.5 bathrooms, and 2,042 finished square feet in the welcoming Oxford Landing community. Luxury vinyl tile flows throughout the traditional interior, which features numerous updates. Step inside the foyer that opens to the spacious combined dining and living room, complete with access to the deck and a convenient powder room. The bright eat-in kitchen includes an island, upgraded countertops, white cabinetry, generous counter space, abundant natural light, stainless steel appliances, and a pass-through window that enhances the open flow of the main level. Upstairs, luxury vinyl tile continues into three bedrooms, including the primary suite with double closets and a private ensuite bathroom. A centrally located full bathroom serves the additional sizable bedrooms. The lower level offers a fourth bedroom and a storage room with laundry facilities. Outdoor living includes an elevated deck overlooking tranquil, wooded views, perfect for relaxing. Additional conveniences include two assigned parking spaces.
