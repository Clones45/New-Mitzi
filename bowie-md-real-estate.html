Bowie Neighborhoods I Know Inside & Out

Fairwood

One of Bowie's most prestigious planned communities, featuring luxury single-family homes, manicured common areas, a community pool, tennis courts, and top-rated schools. High demand and low turnover — expertise matters here. Known for elegant estates and a strong sense of community.

Woodmore

An exclusive gated community in Mitchellville bordering Bowie, Woodmore is renowned for its golf course, upscale estates, and high-profile residents. If you're searching for luxury real estate near Bowie, Woodmore represents the pinnacle of Prince George's County living.

Pointer Ridge

An established neighborhood with large lots, mature trees, and strong community ties. Ideal for families seeking spacious homes at competitive prices with excellent access to Bowie's top-rated schools and commuter routes.

Mitchellville

Bordering Bowie, Mitchellville is known for upscale executive homes, privacy, and easy access to major commuter routes into DC and Annapolis. A first choice for professionals and relocating executives.

Bowie Town Center Area

Walkability meets suburban comfort. Neighborhoods near Bowie Town Center offer convenient access to dining, retail, and entertainment without sacrificing space or quality of life.

Joint Base Andrews Area

As an MRP-Certified agent, Mitzi specializes in helping military families near Joint Base Andrews navigate VA loans, BAH maximization, and tight PCS timelines — with deep knowledge of the Bowie-area housing market.
