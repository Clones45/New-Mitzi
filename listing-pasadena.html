About this home

Charming split-foyer residence offering 3 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, and 1,794 finished square feet just steps from the water in sought-after Green Haven. Ascend from the foyer to the bright main level featuring an open floor plan and laminate flooring throughout. The combined living and dining room flows seamlessly into the crisp white kitchen, complete with upgraded countertops, stainless steel appliances, and a classic white subway tile backsplash. Down the hall are three comfortable bedrooms and a full bathroom, including a spacious primary bedroom. Both secondary bedrooms feature luxury vinyl tile for added style and durability. The expansive lower level offers even more living space, beginning with a large family room featuring a decorative fireplace and walk-up exterior access. Through French doors, a versatile bonus room awaits, perfect for guests, a home office, or a quiet study. A stunning full bathroom showcases an oversized shower with dual rainfall showerheads, and a dedicated storage room with laundry facilities completes the level. Outside, enjoy a fully fenced backyard with a patio, ideal for relaxing, entertaining, or outdoor play. Additional highlights include no HOA, offering more freedom and fewer restrictions.
